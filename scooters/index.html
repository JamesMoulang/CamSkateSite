Scooter Sessions at The Warehouse

Three weekly scooter sessions at Cambridge's only indoor skatepark — coached after-school sessions, a friendly under-10s slot, and an open session for 8+ riders. New to the park? Claim a free taster session.

After School Club

Thursdays 4–6pm — with Future Scooters

High-energy coaching sessions for young people of all abilities, focusing on fun, safety, and progression. Run in partnership with Future Scooters.

Price: £20 pre-booked, £25 on the door
